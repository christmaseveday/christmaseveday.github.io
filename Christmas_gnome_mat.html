Jul 21, 2024 · Victorian Christmas Card Designs. The design of Victorian Christmas cards was a testament to the era’s fascination with intricate detail and elaborate artistry. These cards often featured hand-painted illustrations, lithographs, and chromolithographs, showcasing the technological advancements in printing techniques of the time. Bizarre Christmas Cards. In1885, Louis Prang and other Victorian artists began to design Christmas cards picturing rather bizarre subjects, putting a creepy spin on some of the seasonal greetings. Such designs included turnip men (half vegetable, half man), bloodthirsty bears, and dead birds, a symbol of the cold winter season. The popularity of the Christmas tree throughout the Victorian era typically falls at the feet of Queen Victoria’s husband Prince Albert.
